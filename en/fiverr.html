Fiverr



We know that working on the Internet is one of the most effective and efficient methods to make money from the comfort of your home, there is a wide variety of pages in which you can buy products and services at different prices, but Fiverr isn't simply one of those pages, but in that web you can also offer your services, services ranging from programming to criticism of certain products, through marketing, translations, videos, drawings, in short, there are more than three millions of services offered at Fiverr, according to the same page. Buyers can also make feedback and give their opinions about the product and/or service they've received, which impacts on the "fame" that you'll have as a service or product provider, depending on whether buyers' opinions are positive or negative , other users will be able to see these opinions and help them to decide whether to purchase or use a particular service. Fiverr is available in any country in the world, there are no location restrictions to be a user of this website.



Offer and purchase services


If you want to buy a service, simply type it in the search bar that appears at the top, you can filter the search by price, delivery time, seller level, and your language.

To exemplify this, we've looked for translation services, as you see, aparence wide variety of users who provide this service in addition to the qualification they've obtained with the number of users who have purchased the service. Also appears the price to pay if you purchase this service.



Now, if you want to offer your services or what in Fiverr is called gig, the first thing to do is to click on the button next to the search bar, Start Selling, then you'll have to create the information that your buyers will see of you, data such as availability of time, social networks, languages you dominate, the skills you've for the services you're going to offer, education level and if you've some type of certification for it that you're going to offer, we also recommend that your photo is attractive for buyers interested in your offer, as well as a description of what you offer. At the end of this part, you'll continue in the process of offering your service by filling various fields such as your service category, level of experience and other details that are necessary to offer your service to the user community.

About payment


In Fiverr you've three ways to withdraw your profits, the first is through a PayPal account that will have to go through a verification process and verify that it is really your account, by this means there is a minimum amount of withdrawal, another This form is through Fiverr's revenue card, which is driven by MasterCard and requires a minimum amount of USD 5 for withdrawal, the third form is through a bank transfer for which there is no mention of a minimum amount in the web of Fiverr.



To withdraw your profits, you must go to Selling which appears in the top menu of the web, and select the option Earnings, where you'll see the three options that we've mentioned and when you've profits to withdraw you must choose one of the three options and follow the steps that will be indicated to you at the time of retirement. The payment takes 14 days after you request them, according to the same website, for long time sellers at Fiverr and that their qualifications have been very good, the payment takes 7 days, this is a bonus that promotes the quality of services .



Referral system


Fiverr manages a system of referrals with which you can increase your profits easily, to access the system of referrals you must click on the image of your profile that appears to the right of the page and select the option that you see in green color Invite Friends & Get $5.



Once the referral module has been opened, you can share your link in several ways: by sending it to your email contacts, sending it to specific recipients, or sharing it via social networks. Once one of your friends registers and makes a purchase on the web, you'll receive 5 USD.



Registration

Frequently Asked Questions


Can I use any payment method for each withdrawal?

No, for all the withdrawals you make, you must use the same method of payment for security reasons, so choose well for the first payment, since the same method you must use for each time you want to withdraw your profits.

Should I've studies or certificates to offer any services?

No, in Fiverr you don't need to be a professional or have a certificate for the service or product you're going to offer, if you don't have them don't worry, what you offer can be a talent or one of your hobbies.

How many services can I offer?

The maximum number of services or gigs you can offer is 20, everything depends on your availability, remember that the faster you deliver what you've been entrusted the more satisfied the buyer will remain and your qualification will be better.

What is the minimum and maximum price I can charge in Fiverr?

Many of the gigs have the minimum price of 5 USD, but some, depending on the difficulty and quality of the product or service varies its price, most don't exceed 50 USD, can cost 10, 15, 20 or 25 dollars, there are all prices, technology or programming jobs are usually the most expensive, reaching the maximum price that can be charged in Fiverr, 1000 USD.

Every time my referral makes a purchase, does Fiverr give me 5 USD?

Unfortunately no, Fiverr only pays you the 5 USD for the first time your referral makes a purchase, which motivates even more to have many referrals and get more and more profits.

I don't have PayPal account

Here, after clicking on Sign Up you can select the type of account you want, which in this case would be personal, you can also read the specifications of each type of account and decide which suits you.

Later you will be asked your gender and tastes and then your personal data and finally, they will ask you if you want to associate your bank card, being able to postponed if you want to do so by clicking on I'll do it later, and can use Visa, MasterCard, Diners Club or American Express.
